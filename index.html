A little about where we came from and what we do.

I was born in a small town in Southern Kentucky and grew up two miles outside of a community that was made up of approx. 25 people. My closest neighbors have always been my family. It is one of those counties where everybody knows everybody. I had an amazing childhood. Some of my earliest memories are of opening presents with my sister at my mom's house when we were both still kids. I had the priviledge of learning Tang Soo Do as a teenager thanks to my family. but as soon as I had the chance I started working in a factory and started to build a family the best way I knew. I never really liked school, and I quit High School my Sophmore year. My sister was an amazing student and I admired that she went to college straight out of school. A short couple years after High School I got my GED and took some nursing classes to land what I thought was my dream job until some of our patients where I worked passed away. Then, hoping to make some sort of impact on the world, I went back to school persuing a Law Enforcement Administration degree. I realized 63 credits into a 90 credit degree that this was not my passion and decided to give technology another try. I changed my major to a BS of Information Technology and have not regretted it yet. I worked full time the second half of my degree, so it was a challange, but I managent to get a Presidents and Deans list award a few times and graduated my program September of 2024. I have had the blessing of having a very supportive family no matter what choices I make in life. They have always pushed me in the right direction and given me the chance to work in many different fields over the years as I searched my calling out. Without God, My Amazing Son, Grandmother, Mother, Father, Sister, Uncles, and cousins I would not be in the position I am today.
